Overview

Purpose

Our company strives to make our rentals simple and fun! We want families to bond and smile through their experience at White Water Rentals. Our employees are available anytime to help you answer any questions and concerns you may have. Come rent at White Water Rentals, and you are guranteed to have a smile on your face.

Audience

Our target audience is going to be anybody 18-55 years old. Most people younger or older will not be renting a raft. This age group will likely have jobs/ careers which makes them in a stable situation to rent for a day. Our audience wants excitement and to be thrilled during their experience. We will need to complete their needs by accomodating everyone in a timely manner and being prepared. Our audience will access our site on their mobile device and occasionally their laptop.
