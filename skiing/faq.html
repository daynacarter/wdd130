Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

This page will cover the most frequent asked questions that we get asked. We hope we can answer any questions or concerns that you may have. If these don’t answer your questions, feel free to contact us!

What’s your return policy?

Don’t like your product or got the wrong size? Have no fear! We have a 90 day return policy and can cover your shipping cost to return it. We want to guarantee that you are happy with the products you purchase!

I’ve never skied before but want to start learning! What is the best ski’s for a beginner?

Great question! We always recommend getting shorter ski’s that have a softer flex. Pick out ski’s that aren’t taller than your chest and that should make your first time the most enjoyable. We always recommend getting lessons at whatever resort you choose and be safe!

Do you offer any ski goggles or helmets?

We sadly do not offer goggles or helmets currently. We are sorry for the inconvenience. We only sell skis, boots, and bindings at this time. We however are working on getting those products in later this year! Join our email list to get all the latest updates!

What is your pricing for your equipment?

If you go to our second page, we have all the pricings listed on that page. Keep an eye out for our monthly clearance sales!

Do you offer any snowboard gear?

We only offer ski gear currently! We are working on our snowboard gear and will be releasing it in December 2023! Exciting things are coming!

How long have you been in business?

Great question! This is our fifth year in business and are so grateful for all our faithful customers! You’re the reason we have the best job! Grateful we can serve our customers with the best products on the market!
